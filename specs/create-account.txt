# Multi-Country Business & Identity Verification (Frontend UI)

## Objective

Design and implement a scalable frontend onboarding flow that supports country-specific KYC (individual) and KYB (business) verification processes.

Supported countries (Phase 1):

- Ghana
- Nigeria
- United States
- United Kingdom

This issue covers frontend UI and form logic only. Backend validation, third-party verification APIs, and compliance review logic are handled separately.

---

# 1. High-Level Architecture

## Core Requirement

The registration system must:

- Allow country selection at the beginning
- Dynamically render country-specific identity and business verification fields
- Support both Individual and Registered Business accounts
- Maintain a modular, extensible structure for adding more countries later
